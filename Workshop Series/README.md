# Description

This introductory course is a gateway to the wealth of knowledge offered by the AI Excellence Lab at Blend. Dive into a comprehensive exploration of our AI-based workshops, beginning with the LangChain series—covering essential tools, agents, and chains—and advancing to intricate topics like RAG Systems and Multi-Agent systems. But it’s not just about the technical aspects; this lab series is designed to be engaging and interactive. Each workshop includes quizzes that allow you to test your understanding and solidify your learning after every session.
